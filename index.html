Big Chungus

Objective

Defeat as many opponents as possible.

Controls

Keyboard and mouse

  • Arrow keys | WASD to walk.
  • Move mouse to aim dash. Click to dash.

Touch

  • Left floating joystick: walk.
  • Right floating joystick: aim dash. Release to dash.

While dashing, Big Chungus is invulnerable and will deal damage to enemies. Dash charges automatically, and there is a minimum charge before chungus can dash.
